
Coding Assignment

Description 
You're given two numbers, ​N x M ​ and you're expected to generate 2D array (​N x M ​ ) where each cell is labeled according to their order in a specific traversal. 
See the example for the traversal. 
 
Example 
Input​: 
5 x 5 
 
Expected​: 
1  2  5  10 17 
4  3  6  11 18 
9  8  7  12 19 
16 15 14 13 20 
25 24 23 22 21
